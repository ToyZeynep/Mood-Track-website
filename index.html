Frequently Asked Questions

Is MoodTrack a replacement for therapy?
No. MoodTrack is a self-assessment tool to help you track your mental wellness. It's not a diagnostic tool and shouldn't replace professional mental health care. If you're struggling, please reach out to a qualified mental health professional.
How often should I take the assessment?
We recommend taking the assessment every 2 weeks to track meaningful changes. However, you can adjust this frequency based on your needs.
Is my data really private?
Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't have servers that collect or store your assessment results. Your privacy is completely protected.
What if I get a concerning score?
The app provides resources and recommendations based on your score. If you receive a high score indicating significant symptoms, we strongly encourage you to speak with a mental health professional.
Is MoodTrack free?
Yes! MoodTrack is free to download and use. The app includes optional ads to support development, but all core features are completely free.
